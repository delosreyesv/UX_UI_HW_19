Hello!

I’m Vanessa de los Reyes!

I’m a former college history professor with a love of the fiber arts and a passion for design and research. I want to create a better world and make ideas and products accessible and enjoyable to all.

My skills include: user-centric design, user research, user empathy and engagement, visual wireframing, prototyping, user flows, usability testing, interaction design, user interface, and information architecture.

  • User Research +
    As designers, we might think we know how to fix the user’s problem. But, often our users tell us a different story. This is why user research is an important part of the design process. I do this by conducting interviews, surveys, and observations.
  • User Empathy +
    It’s essential that I understand the user and their pain points and frustrations. To do so, I create an affinity diagram, a user persona, and a user journey.
  • Qualitative Interviews +
    To get accurate and usable data, users have to feel comfortable to make mistakes and to be honest during the research stage. As designers, we have to provide them with a safe space and listen to what they tell us.
